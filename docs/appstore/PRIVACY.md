# Privacy Policy — PokerCoach

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

PokerCoach does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

- **No data leaves your device.** Your bankroll, session history, settings, and
  statistics are stored only on your device (using the system's standard
  on-device storage) and are never uploaded.
- **No accounts.** PokerCoach requires no sign-in and no personal information.
- **No tracking or analytics.** The app contains no advertising, no third-party
  analytics, and no tracking of any kind.
- **No network use.** PokerCoach plays entirely offline.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to request, export, or delete
beyond removing the app, which clears its on-device storage.

Questions: file an issue at https://github.com/MURD0X/PokerCoach
