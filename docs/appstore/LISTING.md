## Description
PokerCoach is the Texas Hold'em app that grades your play.

After every hand, it reviews each decision you made — street by street — and tells you which were right, which were defensible, and which were leaks, all on the numbers that were knowable at the time. Then it names the single mistake that cost you the most chips: the lesson of the hand. You don't just play poker — you learn from every hand you play.

AFTER EVERY HAND, REVIEWED
• Each decision graded: matched the coach, a defensible deviation, or a leak
• Graded on the equity and pot odds that were actually knowable at the time
• The costliest mistake called out as the lesson of the hand
• "Learn more" links jump from any verdict straight to the relevant lesson

While you play, a live coach shows your real win probability, your outs, and the pot odds on every decision — and explains, in plain English, what to do and why.

THE COACH
• Live win % and tie %, recalculated every street
• Your outs, pot odds, and the rule of 4 and 2
• A clear FOLD / CHECK / CALL / BET / RAISE recommendation with the reasoning
• Three coach modes: full advice, numbers only, or off

LEARN BY DOING
• Interactive lessons: a Chen-scale calculator, a pot-odds playground, "which hand wins?", and a position explorer
• Drill mode: ten rapid-fire decision flashcards, scored against the coach
• "Learn more" links jump from any coaching moment straight to the lesson

A REAL TABLE
• Opponents with hidden personalities — tight or loose, passive or aggressive, rookie or expert — that you uncover by playing
• A persistent bankroll, table stakes, and the discipline of knowing when to walk away
• Tournament mode: a sit-n-go with escalating blinds, eliminations, and a prize pool
• Provably fair dealing — a cryptographic shuffle, and opponents who only see what you see

No real money. No in-app purchases. No accounts. Just poker, taught well.
